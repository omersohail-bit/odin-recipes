Banana Pancakes

Description

Banana pancakes are a delicious twist on the classic breakfast favorite, blending the sweetness and texture of ripe bananas with the fluffy, warm comfort of pancakes. The key to making them extra special is mashing ripe bananas and incorporating them into the pancake batter, which not only infuses the pancakes with natural sweetness but also adds moisture. These pancakes are often enhanced with a touch of vanilla extract and cinnamon, which complement the bananas' flavors, creating a satisfying and aromatic breakfast dish. They can be topped with fresh banana slices, maple syrup, nuts, or even a dollop of whipped cream for an indulgent experience.

A great aspect of banana pancakes is their versatility. They can easily be adapted to fit different dietary needs, such as using gluten-free flour for a gluten-free version or swapping regular milk for almond or oat milk for a dairy-free treat. They're also a fantastic way to use up overripe bananas that might otherwise go to waste, providing a healthier option compared to sugary store-bought pancakes. Whether enjoyed as a leisurely weekend breakfast or a quick weekday morning meal, banana pancakes are a deliciously satisfying dish that appeals to both children and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated. It's OK if your batter is slightly lumpy.
  2. Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes (or until each side is golden brown).
  3. Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
